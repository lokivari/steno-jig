貳、指法 | B. Fingering

亞偉速錄鍵位分佈分左、有兩部分，各有12個鍵位，其鍵位碼的設置左右對稱，均採用大寫的英文字母標示。The keys in Yawei steno are mirrored down the center, so that each hand has access to the same keys, and the placement of the fingers should be as follows:

  1. 拇指位於X、B鍵之間 | The thumb rests between X and B;
  2. 食指位於G鍵上或D、G、Z、W鍵的中部 | The pointer finger rests on G or between D, G, Z, and W;
  3. 中指位於I鍵 | The middle finger rests on I;
  4. 無名指位於N鍵 | The ring finger rests on N;
  5. 小指位於O鍵或A、O鍵之間 | The pinky rests on O or between A and O.

參、讀打練習 | C. Exercises

亞偉速錄的基本單位是雙音詞語，由左右雙手同時進行並擊，左手擊打首音節，右手則擊打尾音節。例如，「我的」的打法𤔡「O-D」，左手按O鍵，右手同時按D鍵。
The basic unit of a key stroke in Yawei is the two character phrase. This is stroked by typing the first character on the left hand side, and simultaneously stroking the second character on the right hand side. For example, to stroke 我的 wode "my", the left hand presses O for 我 while the right hand presses D for 的, giving O-D.
