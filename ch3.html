2. 三音略碼詞語 | 2. 3-Character Briefs

常用三音詞語的具體略打方法是：
Briefs for common three-character phrases are as follows:

[首音節碼]/X-X [1st syl.]/X-X

先用左或右手單擊三音詞語開頭的音節碼，後兩個音節碼用左、有兩手同時並擊省略標誌功能碼X-X來略打。現已編定一批三音略碼，存入詞庫。這裡先介紹12個。
To stroke a three-character brief, begin by stroking the the first syllable of the phrase on either the right or left hand, then follow up by stroking X-X. Below are 12 such briefs using the initial consonants introduced thus far.
