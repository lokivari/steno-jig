2. 鍵位碼和指法 | Key positions and fingering 聲碼使用的鍵位碼主要是X、B、D、Z、G五個鍵位碼，個別用到I和U的聲碼，這些都是排列在前面的鍵位碼。構成聲碼的鍵位碼主要靠拇指和食指來進行擊打，有個別聲碼用到中指。
The consonant codes primarily use the keys X, B, D, Z, and G, with some initial consonants also requiring the addition of I and U. As these represent the initial consonant of a syllable, they are written at the beginning of the code. These codes are input primarily using the thumb and pointer finger, with some requiring the additional use of the middle finger.

提示：

  1. 與英文字母發音很接近的韻碼有16個，即I（ㄧ）、U（ㄨ）、E（ㄜ、ㄟ）、A（ㄚ）、O（ㄛ）、N（ㄣ）、IN（ㄧㄣ）、AN（ㄢ）、IA（ㄧㄚ）、IAN（ㄧㄚ）〔其實是INA，但IAN此順序較好記住〕、AO（ㄠ）、IAO（ㄧㄠ）、IE（ㄧㄝ）、UN（ㄨㄣ）、UAN（ㄨㄢ）、UA（ㄨㄚ）。這些韻碼無需特別記憶。
  2. 與英文字母發音有稍微不同的韻碼有2兩個，即UE（ㄨㄟ）和IU（ㄩ），利用IU還可以構成IUN（ㄩㄣ）、IUE（ㄩㄝ）和IUAN（ㄩㄢ）。這5個韻碼，只要理解其構成原理，亦無需記憶。
  3. 需要加以記憶的只有8個：NE(ㄥ）、INE（ㄧㄥ）、IO（ㄞ）、NO（ㄤ）、EO（ㄡ）、UEO（ㄨㄥ）、XE（ㄦ）和EA（ㄧㄛ）。
  4. 其他的韻碼可以用上面的韻碼加以組合構成：UIO（ㄨㄞ）、INO（ㄧㄤ）、UNO（ㄧㄤ）、IEO（ㄧㄡ）、IUEO（ㄩㄥ）。只要掌握了上面的韻碼，這5個韻碼也無需特別記憶便能掌握。
NB:
  1. For those already familiar with Hanyu Pinyin, fifteen of the above rime codes will appear instantly recognizable, namely I, U, E, A, O, IN, AN, IA, INA(="-ian", but with the order of A and N flipped), AO, IAO, IE, UN, UAN, and UA.
  2. Two of the above rime codes represent slight departures from Hanyu Pinyin, namely UE (wei) and IU (yu), the latter of which is also used in combination with other endings, giving IUN (yun), IUE (yue), and IUAN (yuan). One need only remember this rule for these rime, no other memorization necessary.
  3. The remainder of the codes do require some amount of memorization/familiarization, as N(en), NE (eng), INE (ying), IO(ai), NO (ang), EO (yo), UEO (weng), XE(er), and EA (ya) are (in some cases) significant departures from Hanyu Pinyin.
  4. Having memorized the above, learning the codes which use them becomes relatively easy, as the rest merely add U for "w-"or I for "y-" to the beginning, giving UIO (wai), INO (yang), UNO (wang), IEO (you), IUEO (yong).
